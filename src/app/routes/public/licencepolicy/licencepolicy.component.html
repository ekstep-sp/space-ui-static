Licence Policy

 

The EkStep  Foundation (the “Foundation”) believes that open Assets i.e. Asset that is available free and for public use provide immense opportunities for improving access to scarce resources to create solutions and programs for various societal challenges. Open Assets can be reused within the terms of the license and modified within the terms of the license permitted. Source material is given appropriate credit and attribution, in accordance with the terms of its license. Accordingly, the Foundation follows the principles of open Assets shared as public goods on its SPace Portal.

Defined terms that are not defined here shall have the meaning ascribed to them in the Terms of Use.
